Analysis of YouTube videos

The number of videos submitted by minute, compared by the videos that are over 15 minutes, and those that are under 15 minutes.

To create this plot, I used geom_histogram which presented the frequency or the number of videos that were recorded for each minute (e.g. at 8:05 two responses were submitted, so at the 5th minute, we can see one response that represents a video that was over 15 minutes and the other that was under 15 minutes).

The total number of words contained in the title, comparing the by the videos that have just a subject in the thumbnail, and those that have a subject and text in the thumbnail.

I chose to use geom_col to compare the two groups from the visuals in thumbnail, to find the total number of words, from the plot we can see that those videos with a subject and text had a much higher total of words in title than those with just a subject.

The median number of words in the title, compared by channels that are verified and those channels that were unverified.

I chose to make a boxplot where the two groups of channels that were verified and those that were unverified, where we could compare the two median number of words in the title from both groups.
